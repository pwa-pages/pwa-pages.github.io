Welcome to "King of antichess"

This website is for those who are (aspiring) kings of antichess.
Players who play for a while know that the king is a very important piece in antichess.
So I figured king of antichess would be a nice name for this website.



On this website there will be information about events,
and also some resources / links to resources related to antichess.

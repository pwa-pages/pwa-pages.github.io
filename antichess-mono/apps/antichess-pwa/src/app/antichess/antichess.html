The rules of this game are the same as for normal Chess (starting position and piece movement) with the following differences:

  • No castling is allowed.
  • A pawn can promote to a king.
  • There is no check or checkmate.
  • Who loses all pieces including the king or cannot make a legal move, wins the game.
  • Capturing of the opponent's pieces is mandatory — a player must capture if any capture is available; only if no capture is possible may they make a non-capturing move.
